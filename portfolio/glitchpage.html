Glitch Art














My series of glitch images used three paintings from one of my favorite Chinese artists Yichuiwuyue.
These three paintings are plum blossom (Mei), ginkgo (Xing), and Bamboo (Zhu) that have traditional Chinese flavor.
My focus in creating this series of glitch images is to try to blend Chinese traditional style with glitch art
and find the visual and aesthetic balance between these two different styles.
